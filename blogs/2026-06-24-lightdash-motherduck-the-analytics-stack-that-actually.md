Lightdash launched a native integration with MotherDuck, the serverless DuckDB warehouse, letting teams connect their warehouse in about five minutes. The partnership pairs MotherDuck's hypertenancy architecture, which isolates compute per user and agent, with Lightdash's code-first BI layer where metrics, dashboards, and permissions live in version-controlled dbt projects.
